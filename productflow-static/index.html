Product management dashboard for focused teams

Manage product projects, tasks, and hypotheses in one place.

ProductFlow helps product teams organize projects, track tasks, document hypotheses, and keep product decisions visible.

Start with ProductFlow

The problem

Product teams often keep projects, tasks, hypotheses, and decisions in different tools. As a result, context gets lost, priorities become unclear, and progress is harder to track.

  • Project information is scattered across many places.
  • Important decisions are not documented clearly.
  • Teams spend time searching for context instead of moving forward.

The solution

ProductFlow gives teams a simple structure for managing product work: projects, tasks, hypotheses, metrics, and decisions are connected in one dashboard.

Core features

  • Project management

    Create product projects and keep their status visible.

  • Task tracking

    Break product work into tasks and track progress by status.

  • Hypothesis documentation

    Write product hypotheses and connect them with project goals.

  • Metrics overview

    Review key product metrics and understand what changed.

How it works

  1. Create a project

    Start with a product initiative, feature, or experiment.

  2. Add tasks

    Split the project into clear steps that can be tracked.

  3. Document hypotheses

    Describe what you expect to happen and why it matters.

  4. Review progress

    Use statuses and metrics to understand where the project stands.

Why teams use ProductFlow

  • Clear structure for product work.
  • Less context switching between tools.
  • Better visibility for project progress.
  • Simple way to connect tasks, hypotheses, and metrics.
